Sync Miles are convertible airlines miles you can buy using Bitcoin

Miles are usable on eighteen different global airlines like Delta, Jet Blue, Virgin Atlantic, AeroMexico and more. To order, simply select the amount of miles you'd like purchase and the airline and that's it - miles will be credited to your program account within 24-48 business hours.

Miles can be transferred to an account with any of the following airline loyalty programs:

  • United - MileagePlus
  • ANA - ANA Mileage Club
  • AeroMexico - Club Premier
  • Air Canada - Aeroplan
  • Air France - Flying Blue
  • Alitalia - MilleMiglia
  • British Airways - Executive Club
  • Cathay Pacific - Asia Miles
  • Delta - SkyMiles
  • El Al - Matmid
  • Emirates - Skywards
  • Frontier - EarlyReturns
  • Hawaiian Airlines - Hawaiian Miles
  • Iberia - Iberia Plus
  • JetBlue - TrueBlue
  • Singapore Air - KrisFlyer
  • Virgin America - eleVAte
  • Virgin Atlantic - Flying Club
